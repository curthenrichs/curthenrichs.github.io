## Accessibility Policy

_Last updated: July 1, 2026_

This is an accessibility statement from Curt Henrichs LLC for Curt Henrichs' Portfolio website.

### Conformance Status

The [Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG)](https://www.w3.org/WAI/standards-guidelines/wcag/) define requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities, at three levels of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA. This website has not been formally assessed against WCAG 2.1 Level AA, meaning the content has not been evaluated or the evaluation results are not available.
